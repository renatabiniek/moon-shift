Moonlight Shift

How does it work?

  1. Pick a task (only one at a time!)
  2. Set a 25 minute timer
  3. Focus on that task until the time is up
  4. Mark the task as complete on your todo list
  5. Take a 5 minute break
  6. After 4 focused session take a longer 15-minute break
  7. Repeat

Why does it work?

  1. Making it easy to just get started
    Doing something small for a short period of time is easier than trying to take on a big project all at once.
  2. Combating distractions
    Each session is dedicated to one task and each break is a chance to reset and bring your attention back.
  3. Becoming more aware of where your time goes
    You have a clear measurement of your finite time and your efforts, allowing you to reflect and plan your days more accurately and efficiently.
  4. Gamifying your productivity
    Each session is a fresh start and opportunity to improve, challenge yourself to focus, and limit distractions

Tip

Break down complex projects. If a task requires more than four 25-minute-session, it needs to be divided into smaller, actionable steps.
Sticking to this rule will help ensure you make clear progress on your projects.
